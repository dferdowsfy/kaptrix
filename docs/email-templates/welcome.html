What you will see inside

Kaptrix turns an AI company's data room into a structured, reproducible diligence read. Your workspace is a read-only client view of the work your deal team is running. You will find:

Snapshot of the engagement, headline scores, and where the deal stands across the framework.

Scoring & Coverage

Positioning

Report

The polished IC-ready deliverable you can export or share internally.
